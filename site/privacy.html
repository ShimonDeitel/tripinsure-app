Privacy Policy

Tripcase Insurance is an offline-first app. All data you enter (Policy Number, Provider, Coverage End Date, and related entries) is stored locally on your device using the app's own private storage. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of your data with us or any third party. There are no analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs in this app.

The only network communication in this app is with Apple's App Store servers, used solely to process in-app purchases and subscriptions through StoreKit. We never see or store your payment information; that is handled entirely by Apple.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is deleted with it.
